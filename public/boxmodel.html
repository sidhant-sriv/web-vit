Space 🚀

Space is a vast expanse of emptiness. It is a vacuum, meaning it contains almost nothing. It is mostly just empty space between planets, stars, moons, comets, and asteroids. On Earth, space begins at the Kármán line (100 km above sea level). This is where Earth's atmosphere is said to stop and outer space begins. This is not a firm boundary but is a convention used by scientists and diplomats.
